Bhairav yagya & puja.

The Maha Kaal Bhairava Enemy Destruction Mantra is one of the most destructive of the Indian Tantric Mantras for eradicating enemies. This is an out-and-out Tamas Mantra and is not to be practised if you are not an experienced Tantric. The purpose of this Prayog is to invoke Bhairava to destroy your enemy. In the path of life the basic two obstacle creator enemies are certain problems and unexpected accidents. If these main two obstacles are not there in life; a person can have a good hold over the progress of material and spiritual life. The movement of time is infinite and runs continuously. This resulting us in present to term going off as ‘past’ and expected as ‘future’. But every incident is pre-placed in the motion of time and when you reach that particular point of the time it comes in front of you as ‘Present’. This way in our life if any situation is there or arriving situations all those were prearranged in the time as a result enforced with karma. 

Who should do Bhairav   Yagya and Puja?

Lord Bhairav ​​is worshipped in Tantra Vidya. Bhairav ​​is considered to be an incarnation of Lord Shiva. The colour of Lord Bhairav ​​is black and he has four arms, in which he is holding a trident, khaddar, Thapar and around, as well as his vehicle is a dog. He is considered to be the lord of ghosts and yoginis. In Kali Yuga, one has to face obstacles, troubles and enemies every step of the way. In such a situation, chanting mantra remains the only way through which you can get victory over your problems and enemies. It is also described in the scriptures that by installing and worshipping Bhairav ​​Yantra, all obstacles are removed.

Benefits of Bhairav  Yagya and Puja.

  • Shri Kaal Bhairav Pooja is performed with a specific count of chant of Shri Kaal Bhairav Mantra which is the mantra of Lord Kaal Bhairav and this mantra connects the native to Lord Kaal Bhairav.
  • Shri Kaal Bhairav Puja is generally performed for a long age, bravery, courage, victory in fights or battles, removing obstacles, removing black magic or sorcery, removing or getting rid of evil spirits, Ghost bandha, apparition baadha, and fiend bandha, success in some specific business ventures, good health, good materialistic gains and blessings of Lord Kaal Bhairav or some other specific wish.
  • Shri Kaal Bhairav Puja is generally started on a Saturday and it is generally completed on the following Saturday where the day of the start of a Shri Kaal Bhairav Pooja can sometimes change depending upon the time which is required by the Pundits doing this Pooja to complete the chant of Shri Kaal Bhairav Mantra.
