Pitra dosh  Yagya Puja.

In Indian Vedic astrology, Pitra Dosh Puja occurs when there is a conjunction of Sun and Rahu in the ninth house or a bad position of the ninth house or the Sun or the ninth house is under the influence of malefic planets like Saturn, Rahu and Ketu. The concept of Pitru Dosha Puja is held about it being one of the most important rituals to neutralize the ill effects of this dosha.

It is believed that if Pitra Dosh Puja is not performed or due priority is not given to neutralize it, then the life of a person becomes full of different misfortunes.
