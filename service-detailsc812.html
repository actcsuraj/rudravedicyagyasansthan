Vishnu  yagya & puja.

Vishnu Yagya as the name suggests is the Yagya performed to invoke the blessings of Lord Vishnu. He is the perceiver of the universe and upholds Dharma (cosmic law). Vishnu has a very important place in the Hindu religion. He is the nourisher as well as the protector of the universe. To maintain general well-being, this Yagya is very important. All the divine incarnations are ascribed to Lord Vishnu. The Yagya is performed by reciting one thousand names of lord Vishnu twelve hundred and fifty times. People in corporate sectors are benefitting miraculously from this Yagya.
 

Who should do Vishnu   Yagya and Puja?

As we know that Lord Vishnu is one of the trinity of Brahma, Vishnu and Mahesh. Lord Vishnu has taken birth again and again on earth for the welfare of the people. Today we will tell you about Vishnu Puja. Lord Vishnu is said to be the maintainer of the world. When any crisis arises in one's life or on earth, only Vishnu finds its solution. It is Lord Vishnu who has saved the earth from the crisis by taking incarnation again and again. Vishnu ji is also called by the name of Shrihari.

Benefits of Vishnu Yagya and Puja.

  • There is profit in business by worshipping Vishnu.
  • Problems coming in life are overcome by worshipping Vishnu.
  • Worship of Vishnu opens the way to attain salvation.
  • Vishnu Puja is blessed with a long and happy life.

 Special procedure for Vishnu  Yagya Puja.

  1.  Ganeshatharvashisha Recite.
  2. Mangal Shloka.
  3.  Purification. (shudhikaran)
  4. Rakshavidhan.
  5. Mangal Manntra (swastivachan).
  6. Deep, Shankh, Ghanta Pujan.
  7.  Gauriganesh pujan. 
  8. kalash sthapan.
  9. varun pujan. 
  10. navgrah pujan. 
  11. Vishnu  special pujan. 
  12. Vishnu   puja havan. 
  13. purnahuti. 
  14. aarti.

  16. prarthna.
  17. visarjan ceremony end.
